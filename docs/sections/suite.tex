\section{Integration Suite}
While numerous spatial discretization methods exist, this study employs finite difference schemes to reduce the PDEs into coupled ODEs. Once discretized, various numerical integrators for ODEs from the \lib{solve\_ivp} suite in \pkg{SciPy} \cite{virtanen_scipy_2020}, most notably LSODA \cite{petzold_automatic_1983}, and CVODE from \pkg{SUNDIALS} (SUite of Nonlinear and DIfferential/ALgebraic equation Solvers) \cite{hindmarsh_sundials_2005, gardner_enabling_2022, hindmarsh_user_2026} serve as time-steppers. The integrators are benchmarked on a large parameter space to highlight their structural differences.
